COALCOIN

Coalcoin, the cryptocurrency for the production industries.

Whitepaper

Coalcoin is a Litecoin-based cryptocurrency that tries to be a definitive solution to two problems. Coalcoin will attempt to be the standard mining coin for powerful mining teams, Coalcoin is based on the Scrypt algorithms so it will be mined by ASICs and FPGAs. And the second that the currency will try to solve is to be a currency in industries, in business sectors related to the production of raw materials, such as mining companies, energy production companies and refinery companies.
This currency intends to have as its main interest to be simple, basic and effective for dedicated mining, by this I mean mining farms, large-scale mining in a stable currency. We are also working together with GoldCash and SteelCash (in development) to create a currency exchange ecosystem for raw materials.
Coalcoin Specifications:
Block Time: 5 minutes
Block Time Retarget: 144 Blocks(12 hours)
Total Supply: 45 Millons
Premine: 2% (1 for coalcoin and 1 for the ecosystem)
Block Size: 2MB
Algorithm: Scrypt
Block Reward: 53
Halving: 4 years(420k blocks)
Codebase: Litecoin
Ticket: COAL
