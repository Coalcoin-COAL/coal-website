Coalcoin is a Litecoin-based cryptocurrency that tries to be a definitive solution to two problems. Coalcoin will attempt to be the standard mining coin for powerful mining teams, Coalcoin is based on the Scrypt algorithms so it will be mined by ASICs and FPGAs. And the second that the currency will try to solve is to be a currency in industries, in business sectors related to the production of raw materials, such as mining companies, energy production companies and refinery companies.
